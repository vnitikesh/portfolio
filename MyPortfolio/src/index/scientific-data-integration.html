The challenge

The data environment lacked a coherent ingestion and orchestration approach, creating latency, reliability, and operational issues at scale.

Fragmented data sources made end-to-end analysis painful and slowed the time to actionable insight.
ETL bottlenecks surfaced under high-volume jobs and created unstable processing windows.
Operational drift increased recovery costs whenever a pipeline failed or degraded.
Unified orchestration made the process more reliable, predictable, and easier to monitor.
Improved data readiness enabled better scientific analysis and downstream decision cycles.
Lower support overhead reduced the burden of constant troubleshooting and pipeline repair.
