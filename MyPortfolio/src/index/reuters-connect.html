Engineering challenges

Every architectural decision balanced performance, cost, correctness, and delivery confidence.

Scaling millions of records

We partitioned the ingestion path, isolated heavy consumers, and optimized throughput through staged processing and opportunistic caching. The result was a materially lower tail latency and better burst handling.

API bottlenecks

We introduced rate shaping, request coalescing, and connection pooling to reduce contention under load. The change improved servicing efficiency across high-volume traffic and simplified incident response.

Database optimization

Indexing, query rewrite, and read model tuning reduced slower paths and improved cache hit ratios. That made the platform more predictable during business-critical periods.
